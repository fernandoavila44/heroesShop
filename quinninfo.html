HARLEY QUINN:

Harley QuinnHarley Quinn (real name Dr. Harleen Frances Quinzel) is a fictional character created by Americans Paul Dini and Bruce Timm for the DC Comics publishing house. Quinn made her debut in the twenty-second episode of Batman: The Animated Series, "The Joker's Favor," in September 1992 and became a recurring villain on the series, possesses excellent agility and is quick to fight, just surpassed by Batman and the Joker. Her agility is due to being a gymnast and an acrobat.
